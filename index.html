Welcome to Our Telegram Group

Thank you for joining! To maintain a positive and respectful environment, we have established the following rules. Please read carefully and adhere to them.

1. Be Respectful

We encourage open discussions, but all members must treat each other with respect. Hate speech, personal attacks, or discrimination will not be tolerated.

2. No Spamming

Do not flood the chat with unnecessary messages, promotional content, or repeated posts. This includes excessive use of emojis, CAPS LOCK, and random characters.

3. No Offensive Content

Sharing explicit, hateful, or violent content is strictly prohibited. Keep discussions friendly and suitable for all members.

4. No Unapproved Links

Links to external websites, personal blogs, or affiliate programs are not allowed unless approved by an admin.

5. No Political or Religious Debates

We respect all viewpoints, but this is not the place for political or religious debates, as they can be divisive.

6. Keep Discussions Relevant

Please stick to the group's main topic. Off-topic discussions should be taken to private messages.

7. Follow Admin Instructions

Admins work to keep the group organized. Failure to follow admin directions may result in removal.

8. Reporting Issues

If you see someone violating these rules, please report it to an admin instead of engaging in arguments.

9. Consequences for Rule Breaking

  • First Violation: Warning.
  • Second Violation: Temporary mute.
  • Third Violation: Permanent ban.
